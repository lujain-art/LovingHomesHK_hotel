Our Services & Facilities
At Loving Homes we provide a wide range of services to ensure your dog has a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Luxury Boarding Rooms

Private rooms with underfloor heating and cooling, flat-screen TV, and fresh bedding changed daily.

Grooming Parlour & Spa

Full grooming services: baths, breed-specific haircuts, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and spa treatments.

Secure Outdoor Paddocks

Spacious, fully-secured outdoor paddocks for free play and fresh air every day.

Woodland Walks

Scenic guided walks through natural woodland areas — great for exercise and mental stimulation.

Indoor Play Barn

A large indoor play area perfect for all weather conditions and interactive group play.

Chauffeur Service

Stress-free door-to-door collection and return service across Hong Kong.

24/7 Veterinary Care

Our on-call veterinary team is available around the clock for complete peace of mind.

Pet Watch

Continuous monitoring and daily health checks for every guest staying with us.

Private Grass Area

A dedicated private grass space for your dog to relax, explore, and enjoy outdoor time away from the group.
