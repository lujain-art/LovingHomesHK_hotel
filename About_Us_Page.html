About Loving Homes

About Loving Homes

Loving Homes is a premium dog hotel based in Hong Kong. We are dedicated to providing a safe, comfortable, and enjoyable environment for your beloved pet.

Our team of experienced and passionate animal lovers offers a wide range of services, from luxury boarding rooms and expert grooming to woodland walks and 24-hour veterinary care.

We believe every dog deserves to feel at home — even when their owners are away.

Founded by a group of dog enthusiasts in Hong Kong, Loving Homes has grown to become one of the city's most trusted dog hotels.

Our Mission

To provide the finest care for every dog that walks through our doors — treating them like family, not guests.

Our Vision

To be Hong Kong's leading premium dog hotel, setting the gold standard for pet hospitality, safety, and wellbeing.

Our Values

Safety First

Every dog is monitored 24/7 by trained staff and on-call veterinarians.

Genuine Care

We treat every guest with the same love we give our own pets.

Excellence

From luxury rooms to grooming, we maintain the highest standards in everything we do.

Transparency

Daily photo and video updates keep you connected with your dog at all times.

Our Team

Our dedicated team includes certified veterinarians, experienced groomers, trained dog handlers, and passionate animal lovers — all committed to making your dog's stay extraordinary.
